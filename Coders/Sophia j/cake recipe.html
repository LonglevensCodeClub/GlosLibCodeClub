Chocolate cake recipe

Ingredients:

  • 225g butter
  • 225g caster sugar
  • 5 large eggs
  • 100g self-rasing flour
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 2tbsp cocoa powder
  • A little milk

    • For the icing

  • 250g dark chocolate
  • 250g butter sofented
  • 175g icing sugar

    • Method

    1. Preheat the oven to 180°C gas 4. Also grease two cake tins with a little butter
    2. In a bowl beat together the butter and sugar with an elcetric hand whisk ntil light and fluffy
    3. Add the eggs, 1 at a time beating it together after each one.
    4. Add the flour with the cocoa powder and mix into the mixture
    5. Pour a tiny bit of milk until you have a droping consistency. Then divide the mixture between the two tins
    6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or get a skewer push it into the cake and if it come out clean that means the cake is cooked.
    7. Leave to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a rack or plate to cool even more
    8. Melt the chocolate in a bowl over a simmering water then cool slightly.
    9. In a bowl beat the butter and icing sugar together with an electric whisk until light and fluffy. After fold into the melted chocolate.
